13 Ways

This series is inspired by the Wallace Stevens poem, "Thirteen Ways of looking at a Blackbird."

For each piece, I take an ordinary food item and offer a fresh view on it. In thirteen different ways.

Note: The CFL Journal website, on which these articles were initially hosted, is no longer active. Therefore these links take you to an archived version of the website.
