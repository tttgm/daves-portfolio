Stone Soup

This not-for-profit, grassroots publication, which is given out for free, highlights often-ignored issues in food around Aotearoa New Zealand, as well as highlighting people trying to do things in a better way, or to address these issues.

I strongly associate with the goals and philosophy of Stone Soup, and I attempt to collaborate with them and their broader pool of contributors whenever I can. Not only in terms of publishing articles, but helping with other local food- and community-focused projects.
