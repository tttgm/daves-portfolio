I am interested in what people eat, ate, and will eat. Including myself.

As a roaming freelance writer, my interests are primarily in food. How it's produced, how it's consumed, and how it's percieved.

The connection between how we create and share our food is fundamental, but varied, across different continents and cultures.

I have been exploring this idea around the world for the past 5 years.
